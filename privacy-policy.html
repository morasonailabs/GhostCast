Privacy Policy for GhostCast

Effective Date: May 02, 2026

GhostCast ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application, GhostCast (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

A. Permissions and Data Accessed

To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to certain features on your device:

  • Camera: We use the camera to scan QR codes for easy connection and file sharing setup. We do not store or transmit images or videos from your camera to our servers.
  • Storage (Photos, Videos, Audio, and Files): The App requires access to your device's storage to allow you to select files for sharing and to save files received from other devices.
  • Location (Fine and Coarse): We use location permissions to discover nearby devices and to facilitate Wi-Fi connections (required by the Android system for Wi-Fi scanning). We do not track or store your location history.
  • Wi-Fi and Network State: We access network information to establish connections between devices for file transfer.

B. Automatically Collected Information

If the App includes advertisements, third-party service providers (such as Google AdMob) may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (like the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Usage statistics and ad interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate file sharing between your devices.
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience.
  • Provide and personalize advertisements (if applicable).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: For example, when you choose to share a file with another device.
  • Third-party service providers: We may use third-party companies to facilitate our service (e.g., ad networks). These third parties have access to your information only to perform these tasks on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
